## What PupLog Is

PupLog is a mobile application that provides **educational health information** about common dog symptoms. It uses artificial intelligence to suggest a general urgency level and related educational content based on symptoms you describe.

## What PupLog Is NOT

PupLog is **not a veterinary service, diagnostic tool, or substitute for professional veterinary care**. The app does not examine, diagnose, or treat animals. All information provided by PupLog is educational in nature and should never replace the judgment of a licensed veterinarian.

## Your Responsibilities as a Beta Tester

By participating in this beta test, you acknowledge and agree to the following:

1. **Not Medical Advice.** You understand that PupLog provides educational information only. You will **not** rely on PupLog as a substitute for professional veterinary examination, diagnosis, or treatment.

2. **Seek Veterinary Care.** If your dog is experiencing a health concern, you will contact your veterinarian or an emergency veterinary clinic regardless of what PupLog displays.

3. **Report Dangerous Results.** If PupLog returns a result that you believe is dangerous, misleading, or could cause a pet owner to delay seeking necessary veterinary care, you agree to report it promptly to the PupLog development team at: **[CONTACT EMAIL]**

4. **Beta Software.** You understand that PupLog is pre-release software undergoing active testing. It may contain errors, produce incorrect urgency classifications, or behave unexpectedly. Features may change or be removed without notice.

5. **No Guarantee of Accuracy.** The urgency levels (Emergency, Urgent, Soon, Low Urgency) and educational content generated by PupLog are AI-generated approximations. They may be incorrect for your dog's specific situation, breed, age, or medical history.

6. **Data Collection.** During the beta period, anonymized usage data (symptom categories, urgency classifications, response times) may be collected to improve the service. No personally identifiable health information about you or your pet will be shared with third parties.

7. **Confidentiality.** You agree not to publicly distribute, screenshot for public posting, or otherwise share PupLog beta builds with individuals outside the approved beta testing group.

## Emergency Situations

**If you believe your dog is experiencing a medical emergency, contact your veterinarian or an emergency animal hospital immediately.** Do not wait for PupLog to respond. In case of suspected poisoning, contact the ASPCA Animal Poison Control Center at **(888) 426-4435**.
