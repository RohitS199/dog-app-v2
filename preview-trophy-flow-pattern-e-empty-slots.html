Pattern E · v2 (scrapbook)

The act of tapping IS the customization

Watercolor + memory-book treatment. Stickers tilt at their natural angles, empty slots feel pencil-sketched, paper-grain warms the cream, one washi-tape accent on a grid cell. The mechanic is unchanged from v1 — just dressed in PupLog's scrapbook voice.

  1. 1
    Tap an empty slot (the 3rd slot starts empty). Pencil-sketched dashed circle, faint "+" in handwritten ink. Picker grid opens — "Pick a sticker" in accent color. Tap any earned sticker → fills the slot.
  2. 2
    Tap a filled sticker on profile. Trophy detail opens. Tiny washi-tape piece holds the sticker onto the "page", date stamped in a hand-drawn dashed box. Watercolor ★ ribbon stamps the upper-right corner, reads Featured. Tap the ribbon → unpins.
  3. 3
    Tap "View all 12 stickers". Browse mode. Each cell sits at a slightly different angle (hand-placed feel). Featured stickers wear the ★ stamp. One cell has a washi-tape accent — purely aesthetic, not functional.
  4. 4
    Open trophy of an unfeatured sticker. Ribbon is in its dashed-outline "tap to feat" state — same affordance, at rest. Tap it → fills first empty slot. If all 3 are already full, a swap panel slides up showing the 3 currently featured — tap which one to swap out.
  5. 5
    Demo buttons seed states: "Clear all" → 3 empty mounts; "Pre-fill 3" → all slots full (then trigger the swap panel); "Brand-new user (0 earned)" → 3 empty mounts + the picker shows all 12 locked with motivational copy; "Only 1 earned" → 1 sticker auto-featured + 2 empty mounts. Tap any locked sticker in the picker to view its unlock criteria.

Scrapbook tells: sticker rotations vary per sticker (their rotation property from achievements.ts); slot mount has irregular dashed rings; grid cells tilt subtly (~±0.6°); paper grain layer; one washi-tape accent; trophy washi-tape + dashed date stamp + tilted Caveat title. Restrained — angles are subtle, not chaotic.
