Common health concerns for Golden Retrievers
🦴
Hip & Elbow Dysplasia
Large breeds are prone to joint development issues. Watch for stiffness after rest or reluctance to climb stairs.
🌿
Skin Allergies
Golden Retrievers commonly develop environmental and food allergies. Daily scratching tracking helps spot patterns.

Heart Conditions
Subvalvular aortic stenosis is more common in this breed. Energy level monitoring can flag early signs.
