Option 3

Specimen Card on a Cork Board

  • Entrance: Backdrop snaps to warm cork texture (350ms fade). Sticker drops in from above with pushpin landing, 520ms cubic-bezier(0.5, 0, 0.2, 1).
  • Idle motion: Sticker sways ±1.5° on its pin, 5s sinusoidal. Drop shadow tracks the sway (counter-translates and scales).
  • Layout: Sticker pinned upper-center (235px). Field-note paper card at bottom 25%, rotated -1.2°, with thumbtack in corner. "Pinned · DATE" stamp at bottom-right, rotated +4°.
  • New asset cost: Cork texture is procedural in HTML; in RN we'd want a real cork PNG (or layered SVG noise via react-native-svg).
  • Brand fit: Playful, on-brand. Reads as "your wall of wins."
  • Dismiss: Tap anywhere on the cork → pushpin lifts up, sticker exits top, field note fades.
